What Acosta Apps stands for

Three things every app on this page has in common — and always will.

◆

Hand-crafted, not generated

♡

Privacy by default

No tracking pixels. No third-party SDKs harvesting data. What you do in the app stays in the app.

↗

Always free to download

Every app ships free on the App Store. Optional purchases exist to support development, never to gate the core experience.
